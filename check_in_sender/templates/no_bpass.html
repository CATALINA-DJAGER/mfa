Due to technical reasons of Bees Airlines you will get your boarding pass at the check-in counter. Check-in opens hours before departure and closes hours before departure.

Please note, that some airlines charge an additional fee for printing out boarding passes at the airport. To avoid additional costs, we recommend you to approach the check-in counter at the airport with a printed boarding pass.

Passengers who have printed out boarding passes on their own must check in their baggage before check-in ends.

The price of your flight includes unit (s) of checked baggage per passenger up to kg. Hand luggage allowance: up to kg. If your checked baggage exceeds kg, additional time will be required to pay the cost of excess baggage. Keep this in mind when planning your trip to the airport.

Make sure you have all the necessary travel documents and be prepared to show your photo ID.
